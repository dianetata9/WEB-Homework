Humidity

Relative humidity fluctuates throughout the day along with the air temperature. At the coolest time of day, humidity usually peaks. This is because cooler air holds less water, and relative humidity measures the percentage of water in the air compared to how much water the air can hold

The time of day when humidity levels are usually highest depends on the city and ranges from 4 am to 7 am local standard time. According to the above visualisation, humidity was very high.
